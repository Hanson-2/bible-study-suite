📘 Bible Study Suite - User Guide

🚀 Getting Started

Unzip the folder and open index.html in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge).

🔍 Search the Bible

Use the top search bar to search verses or references. You can choose between EXB, KJV, GNV, and NIV translations.

📆 Reading Plan

Track your daily reading using the built-in plan. Click the calendar icon to view today's OT + NT assignments.

⭐ Notes, Highlights, Bookmarks

Click the star to bookmark, use the pen icon to add a note, and color dropdown to highlight a verse.

🤖 AI Tools

  • AI Insight: Type a topic like "grace" or "atonement" for a GPT-style summary.
  • Scripture Q&A: Ask Bible-only questions like "What does the Bible say about angels?"

📊 Charts & Word Frequency

Click the 📊 tab to explore visual graphs powered by Chart.js.

🔮 Symbols, Numbers, Typology

Use the tabs to explore key biblical symbols, number meanings, and foreshadows of Christ.

📜 Doctrines + Echoes

Understand systematic theology and explore thematic echoes between Old and New Testaments.

🕰️ Prophecy Timeline

Track prophecies and their fulfillment from Isaiah, Daniel, Revelation and more.

📘 Apocrypha + Book of Enoch

Use the toggle to enable Apocryphal books and the Book of Enoch for study and cross-reference.

✡️ Interlinear Greek + Hebrew

Study passages like Genesis 1, John 1, Psalm 23 with original language, Strong's numbers, and morphology.

💾 Export + Import

Use the "Export" button to download your study data (notes, bookmarks, highlights). You can re-import it later.

🌐 Deployment

This app is offline-first and can be uploaded to GitHub Pages or Ionos for live hosting.
